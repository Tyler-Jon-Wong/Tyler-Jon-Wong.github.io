Hushed Homes - Hack The North Project

  • Date: Sept 15, 2019
  • Contributors: Max M., Mark Bedaywi, Tyler Wong
  • Github: https://github.com/Tyler-Jon-Wong/Noise-Pollution-Calculator-Res

Description:
The Hushed Homes Project is a web-application that takes an inputted address and outputs possible noise that could occur near the area ex: highways, construction, plane paths, etc. I helped develop it with 2 other team members during the "Hack The North" Competition at the University of Waterloo. The web-application used an online database of helicopter pads and frequent airline paths to determine possible noise disturbances. Unfortunately we could not find any other data to find highways, construction railroads, etc. Currently, the website is down and cannot be visited.
