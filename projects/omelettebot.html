Omelette Making Robot

  • Date: May 30, 2019
  • Contributors: Tyler Wong
  • Github: N/A

Description:
The title here says it all. Circuitry controlled by an Arduino Uno cooks and omlette for you. Toppings are placed in the cups at the top of the box where a stepper motor rotates the cups to drop them into the pan below. Pour the egg into the pan once it's heated and let the bot handle the rest. A 3D diagram of the bot can be downloaded here.
